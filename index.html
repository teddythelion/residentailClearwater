Since its founding in Southern California 26 years ago, Clearwater Residential, Inc. has offered family style living for our seniors, the veterans, and the homeless to persons who are homeless or at risk of becoming so. We currently offer outreach services to all areas in Southern California from our two facilities in Riverside and San Bernardino Counties.

Our Mission

Our goal is to offer family style living for our seniors, the veterans, and the homeless and support services to homeless adults, those at risk of becoming homeless, low-income seniors, and veterans while working to secure housing stability, better health, and the tools they need to resume living independently.


Our staff and volunteers are available to assist, whether you, a client, or a loved one is in need and wants to restart their path toward becoming an active member of society!

    FAQS

    • Can you tell me more about Clearwater Residential, Inc?

      Clearwater Residential, Inc is a homeless program group and an online resource for individuals to connect with peers and mentors that can offer the support that they need to rebuild their lives after finding themselves incarcerated, homeless, or just coming out of rehab. Together we can improve the community in the California area. Join us today!


      Reintegrating into society can be incredibly difficult. Our goal at Clearwater Residential, Inc is to connect you with the tools and resources you need to return to your community and succeed. We connect you with job coaches, assist with life management, and can even find you a mentor for more long-term services.


      Clearwater Residential, Inc is a secure and private online community and homeless program for all people in California. Our city grows stronger when we work to improve the lives of those who live in it and through community mentoring at Clearwater Residential, Inc, we are making a difference.

    • Can you tell me more about your organization?

      The Twin Cities metro area has a population of between 15 to 20,000 Ethiopian and Sudanese people living in their community. These people are often immigrants or people who are underprivileged. Anywaa Community's goal is to unite the Ethiopian Community in the Twin Cities to improve the lives of this population. We connect organizations and individuals who can help the Ethiopian community thrive.
